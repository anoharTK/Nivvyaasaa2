M. C. Narasimha Murthy

Director – Legal, Compliance & Regulatory Affairs

M. C. Narasimha Murty is a distinguished public servant who has dedicated his life to the service of the people of Karnataka. Serving the Government of Karnataka as a Tahsildar, he has played a vital role in implementing developmental initiatives and administrative reforms that have positively impacted countless citizens. Beyond his official duties, Mr. Murty is widely recognized for his philanthropic contributions and deep commitment to community welfare. His charitable works span across multiple sectors — from extending financial assistance to temples, schools, and orphanages to supporting individuals and families in need.

His selfless service has drawn the attention of various media houses, leading to several television interviews that highlighted his inspiring humanitarian efforts. He was notably featured on Public TV’s program “Belaku,” where his initiative to rescue people from a rare illness in a remote village was commended as an extraordinary act of compassion and leadership. Under his guidance and support, significant developmental work was carried out at the historic Shivaganga Temple near Tumakuru, preserving its cultural and spiritual heritage for future generations.

Through his integrity, dedication, and generosity, M. C. Narasimha Murty has earned deep respect across the state — not only as an able administrator but also as a shining example of humanity and service.
